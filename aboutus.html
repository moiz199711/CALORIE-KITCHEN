About Us

OUR APPROACH

While we’re advocates of being kind to yourself and eating food that makes you feel good, we never think that should mean compromising on taste. What’s the fun in eating
otherwise! The contemporary menu offers a healthy mix of continental, Lebanese, Italian, Indian & European cuisine, with a special emphasis on whole wheat and protein rich
ingredients. Our food is not just nutritious and filling but also light.

OUR STORY

We as fitness enthusiasts started noticing that for our clients, eating out and ordering in was becoming staple because of lack of time or resources. We also realized that while they
want to eat healthier, they don’t want to give up their favorite foods. With The Calorie Kitchen, we are helping many fitness seekers realize their goals and making healthy food
taste so good. Opened within the gym, the café offers fresh and healthy options with a unique dining experience. The TCK team uses the highest quality ingredients, and freshly
prepares and cooks your food, ensuring that it meets all your dietary needs.
